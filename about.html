About Us

Randa Company

Randa Electronics Ltd. Sti. was established in 1998 to design and produce products that provide convenience in everyday life. Considering the importance of being a standard and a brand (trademark) in products, in 2008, it registered the “Digimat” trademark. Production phases including designs, software, research and development, and products are done by
